Mel Spectrogram¶

Mel spectrograms extract the frequency information over the course of the song, and display how loud or prominent each frequency is. With this pop song, you can see a large range of frequencies, as pop songs are typically much higher pitched than other genres. https://machineramblings.wordpress.com/2017/05/09/shazam-but-magic/
